Union Budget 2025-26

Know the broad themes — income tax relief for middle class, capex focus, fiscal consolidation. Know India's fiscal deficit target (the government's goal to reduce it to around 4.5% of GDP). Know what capex means in this context: capital expenditure on infrastructure — roads, railways, ports — which has a multiplier effect on the economy.

Key Points

  • • Income tax relief targeted at middle class segments
  • • Capital expenditure (capex) focus on infrastructure development
  • • Fiscal consolidation measures to control deficit
  • • Target fiscal deficit: ~4.5% of GDP
  • • Capex multiplier effect on economy through infrastructure (roads, railways, ports)
