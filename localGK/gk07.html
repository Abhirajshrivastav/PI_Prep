⚖️ Why CJI Tenures Are Short

The Supreme Court follows a seniority convention with a fixed retirement age of 65. This ensures a predictable, apolitical transition but also means you must verify the current CJI right before your interview to avoid an embarrassing error.

📊 The RBI Governor's Mandate

The Governor chairs the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC), which targets inflation at 4% (+/- 2%). This role is critical for rupee stability and foreign investment, linking directly to macroeconomics you'll study in management.

🧠 Analytical Insight: The Era Shifts

1947–64: Nation Building

Nehruvian socialism, institutional foundations, non-alignment.

1960s–80s: Dominance & Instability

Congress dominance, Emergency, fragmented mandates.

1990s: Coalitions & Reforms

Economic liberalisation, rise of regional parties, Mandal politics.

2000s: Stable Coalitions

Managed alliances, high GDP growth, welfare expansion (MGNREGA).

2014–Present: Centralized Mandate

Strong executive, welfare-tech convergence, infrastructure thrust.
